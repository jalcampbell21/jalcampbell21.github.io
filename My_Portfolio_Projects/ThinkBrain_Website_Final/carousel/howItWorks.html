How Our Service Works

It's as easy as following these simple steps to get started with Think Brain.


  1. Sign up as a Tutor or Student.



  2. Login in to your account.


  3. If you signed up as a Tutor, students can find you by searching for tutors in their subject area or their names



  4. If you signed up as a Student, find a tutor by searching for your subject or their names if you already know who you want to work with.



  5. Browse Tutors based on their subjects and see their rate of charge.



  6. Once you've found the Tutor you'd like to work with, click the "Book Me" button to get started.



  7. Get connected and start thinking smart whether it's meeting in-person or over the Internet.



And you're all set and ready to go! Get started going for your goals today.
